About me!

Hi, my name is Trish and I like big cakes. I also like small cakes, cupcakes, pies, pastries... and well, if it has butter and sugar chances are I like it. But what I like even more than tasting those things is baking them. Long before Mix A Lot had a name it was a hobby. It started as a doze cupcakes here or a cookie recipe there. I began to dabble in baking creations for celebrations at my day job, it quickly became a stress reliever. I started daring myself to see if I could pull off certain cakes and testing out and tweaking recipes until they were even better. As it turns out I wasn't half bad. Thus, Mix A Lot was born of creativity, cute aprons and buttercream dreams.

I'd love to make the cake for your next celebration.

Availability varies based on order volume. Early booking is recommended.
